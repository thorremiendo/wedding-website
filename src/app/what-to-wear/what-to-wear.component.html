Theme colors:

Please dress in black, white or a combination of both to honor our black-and-white theme.

Attire style:

Cocktail or semi-formal attire is encouraged, contributing to the elegance of the event.

Please avoid:

T-shirts: Opt for a more formal option to suit the occasion.

Slippers: Closed-toe shoes or formal sandals are preferred.

Jeans: Please choose dress pants, skirts or dresses instead.
